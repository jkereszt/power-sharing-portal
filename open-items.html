Legal

OI-001 Revenue model legality: Does charging a platform service fee violate the "non-commercial operation" requirement in § 42c EnWG? Resolved June 25, 2026: § 42c Abs. 5 explicitly permits professional third-party service providers; non-commercial restriction applies to operators only. We can charge commercially. Tillmann Resolved
OI-003 Pre-contract disclosure UX: What exactly must we show users before they sign up? Resolved June 25, 2026: § 42c Abs. 6 defines exactly three disclosures in Textform: (1) incomplete/intermittent supply warning, (2) need for supplementary supply, (3) possible higher cost of supplementary supply. Plus ongoing notifications when installation stops/resumes. Tillmann Resolved
